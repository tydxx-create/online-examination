How Examination sytem Works

Simple, secure, and efficient examination process from start to finish

1

Registration & Setup

Users register with their respective roles. Administrators set up subjects and exam parameters.

2

Exam Creation

Examiners create exams, set questions, and schedule examination sessions.

3

Take Exams

Students access available exams, complete them within the allocated time, and submit answers.

4

Evaluation & Results

Examiners evaluate submissions, release results, and provide feedback to students.
